## How to Use This Document

This file contains **25 AI project ideas** brainstormed and researched across multiple rounds. Each idea has been scored on 7 criteria in the accompanying Excel file (`AI_Project_Ideas_Scorecard.xlsx`).

### If you're adding new ideas, follow this format:

**Scoring Criteria (rate each 1–10):**

| Criteria | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Feasibility** | How realistic is it to build with current tech and a small team? (10 = can build an MVP in weeks, 1 = needs a research lab) |
| **Market Demand** | Is there a proven audience actively needing this? (10 = people are begging for it, 1 = niche academic interest) |
| **Uniqueness** | How crowded is the space? (10 = nothing like it exists, 1 = hundreds of competitors) |
| **Social Impact** | Does it help underserved communities or make the world better? (10 = life-changing, 1 = purely commercial) |
| **Portfolio & Career Value** | How impressive is this on a resume or GitHub? (10 = instant interview, 1 = generic) |
| **Data Availability** | Can you get the training data you need? (10 = open datasets everywhere, 1 = locked behind NDAs) |
| **Personal Interest** | How excited are YOU about building this? (10 = can't stop thinking about it, 1 = boring) |

**Two combined scores are calculated:**
- **Score (without PI)** = Sum of the 6 objective criteria (max 60)
- **Score (with PI)** = Sum of all 7 including Personal Interest (max 70)

### To add a new idea to the Excel sheet:
1. Add a new row at the bottom
2. Fill in: Number, Category, Project Name, Summary
3. Rate it 1–10 on each of the 7 criteria
4. Add formulas for the two score columns: `=SUM(E:J)` and `=SUM(E:K)` (adjust row number)
5. Re-sort by whichever score column matters most to you

### ROUND 1: Initial Research Ideas

#### 1. Ecosystem Tipping Point Early Warning System
**Category:** Environment
**Summary:** Fuses satellite imagery, water chemistry, biodiversity surveys, soil data, and climate patterns using causal inference to predict ecological tipping points 6–12 months before they happen. Provides specific intervention recommendations at the watershed level (e.g., "reduce nitrogen runoff by 30% in this tributary within 90 days"). Moves environmental AI from monitoring to forecasting cascading failures.

#### 2. Personal Behavioral Causality Engine for Mental Health
**Category:** Mental Health
**Summary:** Builds a personal causal inference engine analyzing longitudinal data — sleep, screen time, social interactions, location, exercise, typing cadence — to identify which specific life factors trigger mental health episodes for each individual. Instead of "you seem depressed," it says "when your sleep drops below 6 hours for 3+ nights AND you have fewer than 2 social interactions, your mood drops within 4 days." Generates personalized, testable intervention hypotheses. Privacy-preserving on-device processing.

#### 3. Neighborhood Equity Intelligence Platform
**Category:** Civic Tech
**Summary:** Real-time AI system mapping block-level disparities by combining streetscape computer vision, public health data, economic indicators, environmental sensors, and resident reports. Designed for residents, not bureaucrats — a community member can ask "Why does my block have higher asthma rates than two blocks over?" and get data-driven answers. Uses federated learning so data never leaves the neighborhood.

#### 4. Metacognitive Learning Coach
**Category:** Education
**Summary:** AI that observes how students learn across subjects — note-taking patterns, where they get stuck, what strategies they try, forgetting curves — and coaches them on their own cognitive process. Tracks long-term retention and knowledge transfer, not just test scores. Gradually makes itself less necessary as students develop independent learning skills.

#### 5. Multimodal Accessibility Conversation Layer for Deaf-Blind Users
**Category:** Accessibility
**Summary:** Combines computer vision, speech recognition, NLP, and haptic feedback to translate complex group conversations into tactile patterns via a wearable. Conveys who is speaking, tone, emotion, social dynamics, and context. Compresses high-bandwidth sensory information into the limited bandwidth of touch.

### ROUND 2: Unconventional Ideas

#### 6. AI Field Copilot for Skilled Trades
**Category:** Skilled Trades
**Summary:** Technician photographs a job site and gets AI-generated task breakdown: parts needed, optimal sequence, local code compliance checks, and safety hazards. Connects to remote experts via video with AR annotations. Makes a 2-year apprentice as effective as a 10-year veteran on routine jobs. Addresses the 300,000 electrician shortage.

#### 7. Biofeedback-Driven Therapeutic Music Generator
**Category:** Music/Health
**Summary:** Generates music in real time based on listener's physiological state (HRV, breathing, skin conductance). Learns which tempos, keys, and instruments bring your specific body toward therapeutic targets. Builds a personal "neuroacoustic profile" and composes unique music that adapts second by second.

#### 8. Endangered Language Resurrection Platform
**Category:** Language/Culture
**Summary:** Community-owned platform combining ASR from as few as 35 samples, video lip-sync capture, voice cloning from elder recordings, and gamified lessons for diaspora children. Communities control validation, translations, and what's sacred vs. shareable. Turns preservation into living intergenerational practice.

#### 9. Hyperlocal Food Waste Intelligence Network
**Category:** Food/Sustainability
**Summary:** Shop owners photograph displays; computer vision estimates ripeness and hours until spoilage. Auto-lists soon-to-expire items on local marketplace at dynamic discount pricing, routes unsold items to food banks. Learns each store's waste patterns and recommends purchasing adjustments. Turns food waste into revenue recovery.

#### 10. Voice-Based Early Cognitive Decline Detection for Seniors
**Category:** Elder Care
**Summary:** Weekly AI phone calls to seniors analyzing speech rate changes, word-finding pauses, semantic drift, sentence complexity, and topic coherence — all validated biomarkers. Builds personal linguistic baselines and flags subtle shifts over months. No cameras, no wearables, no stigma — just a phone call.

### ROUND 3: Personalized to Aryan's Background

#### 11. ATS Bias Shield — Resume Optimizer for International Names
**Category:** Career/Bias
**Summary:** 85% of AI resume screeners prefer white-associated names. This tool detects which ATS systems penalize your name, education country, or non-native phrasing patterns. Shows a "bias risk score" and generates optimized resume variations that preserve identity while passing algorithmic filters.

#### 12. Hajurba/Hajurma Connect — AI Bridge for Grandparents
**Category:** Family/Diaspora
**Summary:** One-button voice messaging for elderly relatives. Grandma taps one button, leaves a Nepali voice message. AI transcribes, translates to English, sends both audio and translation. Timezone-aware delivery. Detects emotional tone shifts across messages to flag potential concerns.

#### 13. Sanskriti Vault — Family Heritage Preservation
**Category:** Culture/Heritage
**Summary:** Point phone camera at handwritten Devanagari notes to digitize. Record elders telling stories — transcribes, translates, tags by theme. Photograph old family photos — organizes chronologically. Builds searchable private family archive of recipes, history, and cultural practices.

#### 14. SafeLand — AI City Navigator for Newcomers
**Category:** Safety/Navigation
**Summary:** Navigation layer for international students combining crowdsourced safety data, real-time transit alerts, and community intelligence. Suggests safe, well-lit, culturally familiar routes. Connects to local buddy systems. Knows "closest grocery store" might mean "store with mustard oil and Wai Wai."

#### 15. Accent-Fair Voice AI ★ (ARYAN'S TOP PICK)
**Category:** Accent/Voice AI
**Summary:** Fine-tunes Whisper specifically on South Asian English (Indian, Nepali, Pakistani, Sri Lankan accents). Released as open API or browser extension. Fixes the 40%+ higher error rate for non-native speakers. Start with 500 hours of Nepali-accented English from volunteers, benchmark against base model. Portfolio project that doubles as infrastructure for millions.

### ROUND 4: Domain-Specific Ideas (Aryan's Areas of Interest)

#### 16. Deepfake Voice Firewall — Real-Time Call Authentication
**Category:** Cybersecurity
**Summary:** Live confidence score during calls: "This voice is 94% likely real." Analyzes behavioral speech patterns — vocabulary quirks, pause cadence, filler words, emotional micro-expressions — that deepfakes can't clone from short samples. Start as Chrome extension for video calls, expand to mobile.

#### 17. Pre-Production Content Oracle
**Category:** Content Creation
**Summary:** Predicts video/content performance BEFORE production. 88.4% of YouTube videos get under 1K views. This AI analyzes channel history, audience demographics, trends, seasonal patterns, and competitor data to tell creators "This topic has a 73% chance of outperforming your average" before a single frame is filmed.

#### 18. Consumer Device Death Clock
**Category:** Predictive Maintenance
**Summary:** Predicts failures in personal electronics and appliances before they happen. Warranty-aware alerts ("Your dishwasher pump is failing and warranty expires in 45 days — file now"). Connects to local repair services, estimates repair-vs-replace economics, tracks recall databases.

#### 19. AI Gym Form Coach — Phone Camera Only
**Category:** Fitness/Wellness
**Summary:** Real-time exercise form correction using just a phone camera propped against a wall. Sub-100ms on-device pose estimation. Detects squat depth, deadlift back rounding, bench press bar path. Builds personal biomechanics profile, predicts injury risk from fatigue patterns. Works with a $200 phone.

#### 20. Cross-Game Cheat Intelligence Network
**Category:** Cybersecurity/Gaming
**Summary:** Shared cheat detection platform for indie studios. Cheat pattern detected in Game A → all connected studios get alert with behavioral signature. Uses transfer learning across game engines. Subscription API giving indie devs enterprise-grade anti-cheat from collective defense.

#### 21. Phantom Inventory Killer — SME Supply Chain Data Cleaner
**Category:** Supply Chain
**Summary:** AI data preparation for SMEs: connects Shopify, QuickBooks, supplier emails, messy Excel files and normalizes into unified inventory data. Then provides cash-flow-aware demand forecasting — balancing "don't run out" with "don't tie up payroll cash." The infrastructure layer that makes other supply chain AI work.

#### 22. AI Dungeon Master — NPC Behavior Engine
**Category:** Gaming
**Summary:** No-code behavioral AI engine. Indie devs describe NPC personalities in natural language; system generates emergent RL-driven behavior. Merchant dynamically adjusts prices. Guard develops suspicion over interactions. Companion forms opinions about player choices. No ML expertise required.

#### 23. Live Jam AI — Real-Time Musical Co-Improvisation
**Category:** Art/Music
**Summary:** AI instrument that listens to you play (MIDI or mic) and generates complementary harmonies, bass lines, or counter-melodies in under 50ms. Learns your style over sessions. Guitarist plays a riff; AI lays down a walking bass line. Ships as a VST plugin for any DAW.

#### 24. Retail Shelf Psychologist — Customer Intent Prediction
**Category:** Retail
**Summary:** Uses existing store cameras (privacy-preserving aggregation) to predict purchase abandonment. Customer lingers, picks up, puts back = different signal than grab-and-walk. Real-time staff alerts: "Customer in aisle 7 undecided on headphones — approach." Feeds into store layout optimization.

#### 25. AI Financial Life-Event Replanner
**Category:** Financial Planning
**Summary:** Detects life events from financial data — new job, moved cities, started freelancing — and auto-triggers financial replans without asking. "You got a 20% raise. Increase 401k to capture full match, pay off credit card in 4 months instead of 8." Quarterly tax optimization for Gen Z freelancers.
